Floor-standing

The floor-standing air purifiers（G series）are suitable for indoor air purification and disinfection of large space, such as hospital operating room, emergency room, outpatient hall, infectious disease department, offices, etc.

SOTO-G3 Air Purifier
Integrated air purification technologies/ Comprehensive protection against airborne pollutants
Smart control system, integrated electrostatic adsorption technology and mechanical filtration, combined air treatment technologies provide comprehensive protection against airborne pollutants.
SOTO-G5 Medical Air Purifier
Intelligent control system/ Multi-stage purification system
G5 is a floor-standing air purifier with strong germicidal effectiveness. It combines multiple air treatment technologies to effectively clean and sterilize indoor air. The smart control panel displays an array of functions and is easy to operate. Contactless hand gesture operation ensures safe and easy operation.
SOTO-G6 Medical Air Purifier
Multiple disinfection technologies/ Washable filters/ Easy to maintain
Constructed with sturdy and durable metal housing, the unit combines multiple air purification and disinfection technologies to provide rapid air purification results. Washable metal pre-filter and electronic cell can be reused to reduce replacement cost.
SOTO-G2 Air Purifier
Destroys bacteria and viruses/ Dual disinfection technologies/ Washable filters
All-metal construction is solid and durable. Powerful fan speed paired with dual disinfection technologies to quickly clean and disinfect the air in a large space. Washable metal pre-filter and electrostatic adsorption filters can be cleaned and reused to reduce replacement cost.
