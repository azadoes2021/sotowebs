      FAQ

      통합공기살균기술이란?

      The integrated air disinfection technology incorporates two different mechanisms to clean the air, electrostatic adsorption and HEPA filtration. The electrostatic component removes most airborne particles to prevent them from clogging the HEPA filter. Therefore, the service life of the HEPA filter is extended, and the cost of filter replacement is significantly reduced. The electrostatic adsorption device can be washed and reused.

      G5 제스처 인식 작업은 근처 인간 활동의 간섭에 취약합니까?

      No. The gesture sensor is purposely installed on the top of the purifier to pick up signals from straight-up, not ones from adjacent human activities.

      G5 청정기만의 특징은 무엇인가요?

      Users have the option of operating G5 without touching any keys, including turning on/off, adjusting airflow volume, saving a specific setting, etc. This feature helps prevent cross-contamination among users.

      The integrated electrostatic adsorption and HEPA filtration technology significantly increased its disinfection capability over other conventional air purifiers on the market.

      The two washable electrostatic adsorption panels can be easily taken out for cleaning.

      유지 관리를 위해 G5를 여는 방법은 무엇입니까?

      Use the provided key to open the maintenance panel. The electrical power will be automatically cut off to ensure safety when the panel is opened. No tools are needed for routine maintenance. After completing the filter replacement, reset the purifier by pressing/holding the flashing filter-changing indicator. The maintenance process is safe and simple.
